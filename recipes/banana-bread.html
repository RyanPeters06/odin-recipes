Banada Bread

Description

This simple banana bread is soft, moist, and full of sweet banana flavor. It's a great way to use ripe bananas and makes a delicious breakfast, snack, or dessert.

With only a handful of ingredients and easy preparation, this recipe is perfect for beginner bakers and can be enjoyed fresh or toasted with butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. Mash the bananas, then mix them with the sugar, melted butter, and egg.
  3. Stir in the flour and baking powder until just combined.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and spread it evenly.
  5. Bake for 45–50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Allow the banana bread to cool before slicing and serving.
